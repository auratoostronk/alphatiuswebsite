Spark Cannon

Primary Fire: Spark Beam

The Spark Cannon fires a continuous hitscan beam at limited range.



Alt-Fire: Explosive Railgun

The Spark Cannon can charge up to fire a concentrated beam with ranges that can extend across entire maps. On impact with a surface or enemy, the railgun explodes.



Alt-Fire Mod: Half-Life Slug

The Spark Cannon's Alt-Fire mode can be modified to fire an even more concentrated beam that pierces through enemies instead of exploding. It also does slightly more damage overall.
