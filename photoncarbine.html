Photon Carbine

Primary Fire: Auto Blast

The Photon Carbine is a energy-based carbine rifle. It fires high-velocity projectiles at a high rate of fire.



Primary Fire Mod: Destabilized Burst

The Photon Carbine can be modified to fire Destabilized projectiles in a 3-shot burst mode. These projectiles deal slightly less base damage than Auto-Blast, but gain power the further they travel.



Alt-Fire: Energy Grenade

The Photon Carbine comes equiped with an underbarreled Energy Grenade launcher. This fires an arcing projectile that explodes on impact. Explosion radius, damage, and direct hit damage increases over distance traveled.



Alt-Fire Mod: Energy Missile

The Photon Carbine's underbarreled launcher can be used to fire Energy Missiles: a non-arcing variant of the Energy Grenade. While it isn't quite as powerful as it's arcing counterpart, it can be detonated prior to impact by reactivating the Alt-Fire button.
