Rocket Cannon

Primary Fire: Velocity Rocket

The Rocket Cannon fires energy-powered missiles that gain more power as they travel. Explosion damage, explosion radius, and direct-hit damage each increase with time traveled. Firing this weapon recoils the user back a fair bit.



Alt-Fire: Rocket Detonator

The Alt-Fire mode for the Rocket Cannon can be used to detonate rockets in the middle of their trajectory. Explosion damage, explosion radius, and direct-hit damage are each increased slightly when this mode is activated.
