Flux Disc Launcher

Primary Fire: Flux Disc

The Flux Disc Launcher uses Flux Energy to fire Ricochet Discs at a high velocity and rate of fire. These discs rip through enemies and, upon hitting a wall, ricochet towards the user's location at the time of the impact.



Alt-Fire: Flux Barrage

The Alt-Fire mode for the Flux Disc Launcher charges up a barrage of 1 to 5 Flux Bombs that follow an arcing trajectory and explode on impact. These projectiles fire at seperate times and follow a strange "zigzag" pattern. Charging for too long causes the weapon to fire automatically to prevent overheatting.
