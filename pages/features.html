Game Features

Alphatius 2 offers a rich and dynamic experience that sets it apart from other arena shooters. Explore the unique features that make this game special!

Weapons & Loadouts

Master the battlefield with a variety of weapons and a fully customizable loadout system.

  • 7 Weapons, each with its own unique Alternate Fire mode, are scattered throughout the maps.
  • A flexible loadout system lets players choose their starting weapon and abilities to suit their playstyle.
  • 32 Weapon Modifiers allow players to alter their weapon’s functionality for dynamic gameplay.

Abilities

Gain the edge in combat with travel, action, and passive abilities.

  • 5 Travel Abilities: Evade attacks or traverse maps faster.
  • 6 Action Abilities: Deploy decoys, bounce energy discs, heal teammates, create forcefields, and more.
  • 6 Passive Abilities: Encourage unique playstyles and strategies.

Maps

Battle across 20 unique maps designed for thrilling combat.

  • 14 Deathmatch maps for fragging mayhem.
  • 6 Duel maps for competitive 1v1 action.

Gameplay Mechanics

Innovative mechanics create a fresh and engaging arena shooter experience.

  • A unique ammo system where weapon energy recharges after a few seconds of trigger discipline.
  • 4 special pickups to turn the tide of battle, integrating seamlessly with the new mechanics.

Learn & Practice

New to the game? No problem. Alphatius 2 provides tools to help you master the basics.

  • A built-in tutorial guides new players through the core gameplay mechanics.
  • The Firing Range map allows players to test weapons and abilities in a controlled environment.
