LPHATIUS

Getting Started

What you will need

  • A copy of DOOM II: Hell on Earth
  • Zandronum: A multiplayer source port for playing DOOM
  • Doomseeker: for browsing servers online (optional)
  • Alphatius

Once you have installed these programs, simply drag and drop alphatiusdm1.11release.pk3 onto Zandronum. Make sure you click the "OpenGL" checkbox. From there, you can play offline with bots, customize your loadouts, and configure other options to your liking.

To play online, you must use Doomseeker and type "Alphatius" in the search bar.
