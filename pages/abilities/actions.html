LPHATIUS

Action Abilities

Regeneration Zone

Spawns a healing zone at the player's feet that lasts 4 seconds. Heals enemies and allies alike.



Power Brace

Reduces incoming damage by 80% for a brief moment.



Disruption Screen

Throws out a device that slows nearby players, disables their travel ability, and removes any movement speed increases while near it.



Decoy Hologram

Sends out a decoy that looks like the player using it. The decoy travels in a straight line, climbs steps, falls off of ledges, cannot be hit by projectiles or hitscan attacks, and disappears after a short while.
