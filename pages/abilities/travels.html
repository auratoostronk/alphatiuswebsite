LPHATIUS

Travel Abilities

Thruster Boots

Thrusts the user laterally in one of 8 directions depending on user input. Stores up to 2 charges.



Jump Thrust

Thrust the user upwards and outwards based on their pitch. Aiming higher results in a stronger upwards thrust.



Warp Drive

Stealths the user and makes the user invulnerable, thrusts them forward quickly, then stops them, reveals them, and removes their invulnerability.



Hyperdrive

Passively increases the users movement speed. Activating increases the user's movement speed drastically for a brief moment.
