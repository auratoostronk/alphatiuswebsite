LPHATIUS

Photon Carbine

Primary Fire: Auto Blast

Fully automatic projectile-based carbine rifle with a high rate of fire and high projectile speed



Primary Fire Mod: Mach Rounds

Fires hitscan attacks at a high rate of fire. Continuously firing reduces the weapons accuracy rapidly.



Alt-Fire: Energy Grenade

Fires an explosive arcing projectile that explodes on impact. Explosion damage and radius increases with travel time.



Alt-Fire Mod: Energy Missile

A non-arcing variant of Energy Grenade with less damage. Reactivate to detonate.
