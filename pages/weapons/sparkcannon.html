Spark Cannon

Primary Fire: Spark Beam

Fires a mid-ranged continuous rail attack beam.



Primary Fire Mod: Siphon Beam

A shorter-ranged variant of Spark Beam. Refunds energy cost on successful hits.



Alt-Fire: Explosive Railgun

Unleashes a powerful rail attack after a very short delay. Explodes at the spot it hits.



Alt-Fire Mod: Piercing Railgun

Unleashes a powerful rail attack after a very short delay. This railgun can hit multiple aligned targets. Deals slightly more damage than Explosive Railgun.
