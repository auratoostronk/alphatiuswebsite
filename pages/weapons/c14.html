C14

Primary Fire: Mini-Cannon

The C-14 uses radioactive energy to fire small arcing projectiles at a moderate rate of fire. These projectiles explode on contact, and push enemies hit backwards. Like most explosive projectiles, hitting a target directly deals increased damage. Firing this weapon also recoils the user back a bit.



Alt-Fire: Gas-id Canister

The C-14 comes equiped with an underbarreled canister launcher. These canisters release acidic gases that damage enemies who walk near it.



Alt-Fire Mod: Gamma Beam

The Alt-Fire mode for the C14 can be modified to fire a powerful Gamma beam that leaves a lingering trail, damaging any enemy who walks through it.
