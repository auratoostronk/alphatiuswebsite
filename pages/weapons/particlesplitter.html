LPHATIUS

Particle Splitter

Primary Fire: Energy Pellets

Fires a spread of projectile pellets. Damage reduces slightly after a set distance, and projectiles dissipate a set distance after that.



Alt-Fire: Particle Cone

Fires a single pellet. Release Alt-Fire to detonate it into a spread of pellets similarly to the Primary fire, but increased damage.



Alt-Fire Mod: Half-Life Slug

Fires a single slug with strong damage falloff. If it kills an enemy, it decays in a violent explosion, blasting more Slugs in a lateral expanding ring. These slugs can chain-react on kill.
