Particle Splitter

Primary Fire: Energy Pellets

The Particle Splitter is a projectile-based shotgun that fires a spread of energy pellets. The pellets do little damage themselves, and lose power after a set distance; however, the weapon fires enough pellets to kill targets at close range.



Alt-Fire: Particle Cone

The Particle Splitter's Alt-Fire mode fires a single pellet that can be detonated into a spread of multiple pellets. This detonation can be triggered shortly after the shot leaves the barrel by pressing the Alt-Fire key again.



Alt-Fire Mod: Half-Life Slug

The Particle Splitter 's Alt-Fire mode can be modified to fire an unstable Half-Life Slug that quickly loses energy upon leaving the barrel. However, if it kills an enemy, it violently detonate into multiple copies of itself in lateral directions. These slugs can "chain-react" to kill multiple enemies.
