What is Alphatius?

Alphatius is a mod for DOOM II, which started out as an idea inspired by Seidolon's DOOM ULTIMATE LASER ARENA. Contrary to the chaotic atmosphere of DULA, ALPHATIUS was designed with balance in mind, while also keeping the fun, fast-paced and action-packed gameplay of DULA and DOOM II.
