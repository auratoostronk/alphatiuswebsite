Tesla SMG

Primary Fire: Plasma Projectiles

The Tesla SMG fires electric projectiles at a low velocity and high rate of fire. Firing this weapon pulls the user forwards slightly.



Alt-Fire: Tesla Taser

The Tesla SMG has built-in electric blades that release lethal amounts of electricity between them. These blades can be jabbed into an enemy to eliminate them instantly before going on cooldown. Firing in this mode also thrusts the user forwards a slight amount.
